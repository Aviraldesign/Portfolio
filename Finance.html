Finance App UI - Case Study

This project focuses on creating a minimal, user-friendly interface for a finance management mobile application, improving usability and visual appeal.

Problem Statement

Users often struggle with cluttered dashboards and confusing data visuals in finance apps. The goal was to simplify while keeping functionality intact.

My Role

  • UI/UX Designer
  • Interaction Design
  • Visual Design & Prototyping

Process

  1. User research & persona creation
  2. Wireframing (low to high fidelity)
  3. Prototyping in Figma
  4. User testing and iteration

Results

Reduced cognitive load, increased retention by 25%, and created a visually consistent design system.
